I am Sascha, designer and creator of stories.

My name is Sascha and I am a designer with a curious brain. I like well crafted stories, badass music, traveling the world and not knowing what will happen. I like to understand how things work and why they sometimes drive us crazy. I am a big fan of life-hacking and saving the day with duct tape. This might explain why my focus lies on web and mobile apps. As long as users can accomplish something with it, I can try and make it better.

Over the past decade I worked with some of the world’s most renowned companies and agencies but also help startups with high hopes and relentless ambition. Five years ago he dabbled into the startup scene, founded my first venture (guestly, an event management app), failed, cried, read a lot and found stories to be an interesting space to explore.

Now I am bootstrapping my second venture, Untold, a storytelling startup trying to make paid journalism work. Scoll on for a fancy GIF...





visit untold.st


Untold is the second step in our mission to find a viable income source for journalists. Having no cash, we started out with a crow-funding platform where authors could pitch stories for readers to pre-finance. It worked enough to get 3 stories funded and journalists paid. So people are willing to pay for good stories online. That proved the first and most important assumption.
Now, with Untold we are learning about storytelling formats and what people will pay for.
Our stories are immersive and a bit different every time.
